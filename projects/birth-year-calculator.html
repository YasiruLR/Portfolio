How to Use

1

Enter Your Age

Input your current age in the "Your Current Age" field. The application validates that it's a realistic age between 0-150.

2

Adjust Year (Optional)

Optionally modify the current year field. It defaults to the actual current year but can be customized.

3

Calculate

Click "Calculate Birth Year" button or simply press Enter. The result appears instantly with a beautiful animation.

4

View Results

See your calculated birth year with additional information and use the Reset button to start over.
